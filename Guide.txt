Launch twitchmoments (NOT TWITCHMOMENTSV3)
An Authorize Button should pop up. Press Authorize. Follow the steps. At the end it should say Authorization has been Successful.
Ctrl + Alt + L is the Hotkey to create a clip. You should hear a small beep sound.
Refresh the main client to see your newly created clips.

if the app appears white when you open it, go to View on the top left, and Force Reload. It should appear back as normal.
